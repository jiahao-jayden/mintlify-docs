---
title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
description: "Quick answers to common questions about coding requirements, page types, target users, and content updates."
---

1. **Do I need to know how to code to use We0.ai?** No. Users collaborate with AI through natural language to complete the website building process — perfect for users with zero coding experience.
2. **Does We0.ai only generate static pages?** No. It generates full-stack websites with frontend, backend, and CMS capabilities — it's not just a static page generator.
3. **Who is We0.ai best suited for?** It's ideal for individuals and teams who need to quickly build corporate sites, brand sites, landing pages, content sites, and service showcase sites — especially entrepreneurs, small businesses, and individual creators.
4. **Can I update website content after going live?** Yes. With the CMS backend, you can continue updating content and managing daily operations after launch without needing a developer for every change.
