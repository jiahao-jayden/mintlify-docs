### **1. Domain Options**

    - Free
    - No domain registration needed
    - Ready to publish immediately
  </Column>
  <Column>
    #### **Domain Selection** (1380 plan only)

    - Includes `custom domain selection`
    - Ideal for users who want a domain closer to their brand name
  </Column>
  <Column>

    - Check pricing first
    - Contact customer service to purchase
    - Use for official binding and publishing after purchase

### **2. Pre-Publish Checklist**

<Note>
  Before publishing, confirm:

  <Columns cols={2}>
    <Column>
      - Page content is complete
      - Mobile display is working properly
    </Column>
    <Column>
      - Forms, buttons, and contact info are functional
      - CMS backend basic configuration is done
    </Column>
  </Columns>
</Note>

<Warning>
  Clicking "Publish Website" will consume one publish credit. After publishing, changes made in the preview page won't sync to the live version automatically — you'll need to click publish again to sync. (CMS backend changes are not affected.)
</Warning>

---

### **3. Publishing Process**

After selecting your domain, simply click "Publish Website."

- No need to purchase or configure your own server
- No need to deploy the project yourself (AI handles deployment automatically)
- Go live directly

---

### **4. After Publishing**

Once published, your website is immediately accessible via the corresponding domain.

Deployment URL: This is your website link. (You can access your site through this link — append `/cms` to the URL to access the management backend.)
