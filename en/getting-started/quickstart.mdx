<Tip>
  **If this is your first time using We0.ai, follow these steps to complete your first experience**
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Define Your Website Goals">
    <Columns cols={2}>
      <Column>
        **Think through four key questions:**

        - Is your website for brand showcase, lead generation, or content operations?
        - Who is your target audience?
        - What action do you want visitors to take?
        - What pages do you need?
      </Column>
      <Column>
        **Common pages include:**

        - Homepage
        - About Us
        - Products or Services
        - Case Studies or Customer Results
        - Blog or Content Pages
        - Contact Us
      </Column>
    </Columns>

    <Tip>
      **Once you're clear, click "Start Your Custom Journey" to begin filling in the questionnaire (website requirements)**
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Write Your Initial Requirements">
    <Note>
      **We recommend writing your requirements as a combination of "goal + style + pages + features + conversion actions".**
    </Note>

    > Example:\
    > Targeting global high-net-worth clients, build a [WeiLing Luxury] high-end foreign trade brand presence, emphasizing [luxury quality and brand identity]; style: minimalist, premium, internationalized luxury; pages include [brand homepage, product catalog, about us, inquiry page]; support multilingual, product category display; [conversion focused on collecting contact information, online inquiries, and facilitating orders].

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-8.png)
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
      If you already have an existing website, PPT, brochures, or copy materials, we recommend organizing them first and [**using Intent Recognition**](/en/building/intent-recognition) to send them to the AI
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Generate Initial Design Draft">
    <Info>
      We recommend confirming the overall direction first — **don't click "Confirm Design" yet**. Make refinements in the design draft stage.

      The platform will generate an initial design based on your input, typically covering:

      - Website page structure (layout)
      - Website functionality
      - Visual direction (UI main visual)
      - Basic website content
    </Info>

    > Example: Review the overall content first, don't click confirm design yet
    >
    > <Frame>
    >   ![0 1](/images/0-1.png)
    > </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Refine Website Content">
    <Tip>
      At this stage, you can continue to add or modify:\\

      <Columns cols={2}>
        <Column>
          **Add content:**

          - Brand name and introduction
          - Product or service descriptions
          - User value and advantages
          - Contact info and conversion entry points
          - Additional pages (enrich your site)
          - Multi-language toggle (e.g., Chinese-English)
        </Column>
        <Column>
          **Modify content:**

          - Page layout
          - UI visual style
          - Images, logo, and visual assets
          - Text content
          - Page responsiveness (recommend adding mobile adaptation)
        </Column>
      </Columns>
    </Tip>
