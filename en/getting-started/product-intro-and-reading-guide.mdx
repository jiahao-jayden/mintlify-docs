## **What is We0.ai?**

<Tip>
  We0.ai is an AI-powered website building team. Simply describe your goals and requirements, and the platform coordinates multiple AI agents to handle the key tasks in building a website — including requirement analysis, page planning, visual design, code generation, SEO configuration, domain binding, and deployment.
</Tip>

- AI-Powered Website Building: Describe your needs in natural language — no coding required
- Multi-Agent Collaboration: Tasks are completed by specialized agents for leadership, design, development, and operations
- Full-Stack Delivery: Generates not just pages, but complete websites with CMS backend capabilities
- AI SEO Friendly: Supports SSR, meta optimization, structured data, and search engine-friendly configuration
- Domain & Publishing: Supports domain binding and one-click deployment without server setup
- Ongoing Operations: Supports continuous content updates, structural optimization, and daily maintenance after launch
