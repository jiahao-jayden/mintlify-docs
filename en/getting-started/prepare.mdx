---
title: "Pre-Build Preparation Checklist"
description: "A checklist of brand materials, visual assets, and basic information to prepare before building your site."
---

<Tip>
  To make the website building process smoother, we recommend preparing the following materials before you start:

  - Brand name and a one-line introduction
  - Logo, brand colors, and visual preferences
  - Product or service description
  - Target customer profile
  - Competitor or reference websites
  - Existing website or legacy materials
  - Contact information and social media links
  - Domain information
</Tip>

<Note>
  You can start even without complete materials, but the more complete your materials are, the closer the AI-generated results will be to your expectations.
</Note>
